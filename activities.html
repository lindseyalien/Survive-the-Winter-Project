Hit the Town

The National Gallery

  • Location: Trafalgar Square (1.2 miles away from UCL campus)
  • Opening times: 10am-6pm daily and 10am to 9pm on Friday
  • Price: Free

The National Theatre

  • Location: Southbank (1.6 miles away from UCL campus)
  • Opening times: 9am-11pm daily
  • Price: depends on the performance

Winter Wonderland

  • Location: Hyde Park (1.7 miles away from UCL campus)
  • Opening times: November-January 10am-10pm daily (except Christmas day)
  • Price: Free entry (rides and shops vary)

Tate Modern

  • Location: Southbank (2.2 miles waway from UCL campus)
  • Opening times: 10am-6pm Sunday-Thursday and 10am-10pm Friday-Saturday
  • Price: Free (specific exhibitions require paid tickets)

Barbican Theatre

  • Location: Morgate (2.2 miles waway from UCL campus)
  • Opening times: 9am-11pm Monday-Saturday and 11am-11pm Sunday
  • Price: depends on the performance

Somerset House Ice-Skating

  • Location: (1.3 miles miles away from UCL campus)
  • Opening times: November-January 10am-10pm daily
  • Price: £7.50-£17
